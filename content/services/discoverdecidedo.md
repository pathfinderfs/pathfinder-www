<p>Life rarely moves in straight lines. Our goals, circumstances and priorities are always shifting. This requires thinking far beyond risk analysis, model portfolios and financial planning strategies. It requires creativity, expertise and discipline matched with deep personal relationships centered on a singular priority: helping you harness your resources to live your most rewarding life.<br/>
<a href="/images/ddd-cycle.png"><img alt="" class="alignright" height="165" src="/images/ddd-cycle.png" title="ddd-cycle" width="300"/></a><br/>
<strong>Discover</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Discover your hopes and expectations for the future</li>
<li>Explore your personal, family and community values</li>
<li>Identify life transitions that you are and expect to be experiencing</li>
<li>Gather and organize your data and documents</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Decide</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Triage your short and long-term goals and aspirations to address your most critical needs first</li>
<li>Illuminate the potential tradeoffs of financial decisions and how they relate to your goals</li>
<li>Explain the concepts you need to make informed decisions</li>
<li>Develop an investment policy that attempts to balance your goals, risk capacity, risk tolerance and market stance</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Strategize and coordinate with your other advisors (e.g., attorneys, accountants, insurance agents, etc.)</li>
<li>Help implement financial decisions and navigate life transitions</li>
<li>Effect the investment policy
<ul>
<li>Investment selection and trade execution on a nondiscretionary basis</li>
<li>Re-balance the portfolio when appropriate</li>
<li>Re-allocate the portfolio due to changes in market valuations or your goals</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Provide performance reports upon request</li>
</ul>
