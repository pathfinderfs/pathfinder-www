<h3><a href="/services/discoverdecidedo/">Discover/Decide/Do</a></h3>
<p>Retirement, selling a business, receiving an inheritance are a few of the major life transitions which attract financial sharks and from which mistakes are hard to recover. Discover/Decide/Do is our way of partnering with you in a long term continuous relationship to help you navigate these treacherous waters and reach your desired destinations.</p>
<h3><a href="/services/investment-supervision-only/">Investment Supervision Only</a></h3>
<p>In between major life transitions, you may enjoy and feel confident in performing your own planning but don’t want the hassle of managing your investments. Investment Supervision Only follows the same <a href="/investment-strategy/">investment strategy</a> as our Discover/Decide/Do service with the following caveat. Because we are not engaged in continuing financial planning, we are not able to properly assess your risk capacity and instead must focus our risk management on your stated time frame and volatility tolerance.</p>
