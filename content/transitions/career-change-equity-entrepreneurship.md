A career move can change much more than your paycheck. New compensation structures, concentrated stock positions, retirement account decisions, and variable cash flow can all arrive at once. We help clients slow the decision-making down, understand the tradeoffs, and move forward with a plan that fits the life they are building.

## When this transition gets complicated

- You are evaluating salary, bonus, deferred compensation, or equity incentives together.
- You have RSUs, ISOs, NSOs, or ESPP shares and want to avoid unnecessary tax surprises.
- You need to decide what to do with an old 401(k), pension, or other workplace benefits.
- You are moving from a predictable paycheck into irregular income as a founder, consultant, or business owner.

## How Pathfinder can help

- Review compensation and equity decisions in the context of taxes, cash flow, and long-term goals.
- Help you evaluate concentration risk when company stock becomes a meaningful part of your wealth.
- Coordinate rollover and investment decisions so your old accounts still support your broader plan.
- Build a planning framework for reserves, retirement savings, and owner compensation if you are starting or buying a business.

## Questions we often help clients answer

- How much company stock is too much for my balance sheet?
- Should I hold, exercise, sell, or diversify equity compensation?
- What should I do with my old retirement plan?
- How much cash should I keep on hand if my income becomes less predictable?
