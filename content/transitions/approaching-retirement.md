The years leading up to retirement often carry some of the most important financial decisions you will make. Questions about timing, portfolio risk, spending, Social Security, and healthcare all become more immediate. We help clients test the plan they have, identify the tradeoffs ahead, and retire with a clearer sense of what is sustainable.

## When this transition gets complicated

- A strong savings history still leaves uncertainty about when work becomes optional.
- Portfolio risk that felt manageable before retirement may feel different when withdrawals are close.
- Social Security, Medicare, pensions, and part-time work all affect the timing decision.
- Large one-time choices, such as paying off debt or helping family, can change the retirement picture.

## How Pathfinder can help

- Stress-test retirement timing under different market, spending, and longevity assumptions.
- Align your investments with the spending horizon in front of you rather than a generic risk label.
- Estimate how healthcare, taxes, and withdrawals will interact once paychecks stop.
- Help you connect retirement decisions to the broader life you want to build in the years ahead.

## Questions we often help clients answer

- When can I retire without guessing?
- How much market risk should I still be taking?
- When should I claim Social Security?
- How do I turn accumulated assets into sustainable spending?
