<p>These are books Pathfinder has recommended over the years because they help readers think more clearly about behavior, investing, and what money is actually for. This is not meant to be an exhaustive list. It is a short shelf of books worth spending time with.</p>

<div class="book-grid">
  <article class="card book-card">
    <img src="/images/books/your-money-and-your-brain.jpg" alt="Cover of Your Money and Your Brain">
    <h2>Your Money and Your Brain</h2>
    <p class="book-author">Jason Zweig</p>
    <p>A strong look at the way fear, reward, and overconfidence show up in investment decisions.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card book-card">
    <img src="/images/books/four-pillars-of-investing.jpg" alt="Cover of The Four Pillars of Investing">
    <h2>The Four Pillars of Investing</h2>
    <p class="book-author">William J. Bernstein</p>
    <p>Useful for readers who want a grounded case for diversification, costs, and long-term discipline.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card book-card">
    <img src="/images/books/stumbling-on-happiness.jpg" alt="Cover of Stumbling on Happiness">
    <h2>Stumbling on Happiness</h2>
    <p class="book-author">Daniel Gilbert</p>
    <p>Not a finance book, but a very helpful reminder that people are not especially good at predicting what will make them happy later.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card book-card">
    <img src="/images/books/your-money-or-your-life.jpg" alt="Cover of Your Money or Your Life">
    <h2>Your Money or Your Life</h2>
    <p class="book-author">Vicki Robin and Joe Dominguez</p>
    <p>A classic for readers who want to think more carefully about spending, work, and the relationship between money and values.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card book-card">
    <img src="/images/books/seven-stages-of-money-maturity.gif" alt="Cover of The Seven Stages of Money Maturity">
    <h2>The Seven Stages of Money Maturity</h2>
    <p class="book-author">George Kinder</p>
    <p>Best for readers interested in the emotional and psychological side of financial decisions.</p>
  </article>
</div>
